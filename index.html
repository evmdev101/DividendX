// DividendX Treasury Cycle
1
SAC Closes → Treasury Funded
All sacrifice proceeds move to the treasury. Capital is ready to deploy at launch. Accepts PLS only over a 5-day window.
2
Capital Deployed into DeFi Farms
Funds enter DivX farm positions across PulseChain — only using purchased coins, never minted tokens.
3
Yield Harvested Daily
Farm rewards are harvested. Yield compounds the treasury base and funds revenue share for DivX stakers.
4
Treasury Buys & Burns DivX
Harvested yield buys DivX back on the open market and permanently burns it — real, ongoing buy pressure.
5
Rewards Paid to Single-Stakers
Single-stake DivX to earn pHEX rewards. The cycle then repeats — buy, burn, reward, on loop.
🏦
Active From Day One
The treasury deploys at launch and staking goes live — no waiting period, stakers can earn from day one.
📈
Rewards Grow With the Treasury
As the treasury generates cash flow, buy-and-burn and staking rewards are designed to scale over time.
🔥
No Minting — Only Purchased Coins
The treasury only uses coins it has purchased for liquidity and farming. Zero dilution. Fair to every holder.
